About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

Our legacy commenced in 1954 and spans over decades, rooted in the visionary leadership of the time.

A New Era

In July 2005, Mr. Abdul Wahab founded Logistics Management International (LMI) and the journey continues.

Key Highlights:
  • Founded by Mr. Abdul Rahim with a single tanker
  • Evolved into Mehran Tankers, a renowned business entity
  • LMI established in 2005 by Mr. Abdul Wahab
  • Guided by "Professional Par Excellence" motto
OUR VISION

To be regarded as the most professional organization in the business, we ensure our core values are met at all times in an ever-challenging and changing corporate market. We make all possible endeavors to stay focused on building an edifice that is exclusive in professional excellence and continuously seeks introspection, thereby manifesting our vision in providing superior logistic services.

OUR MISSION

Expand our business frontiers by discovering new markets, continue to build a performance-driven culture and sustainability, and ensure our clients are provided with customized solutions in Logistics with a concentration on Oil, Liquid, and Dry Cargo, containerized or otherwise.
